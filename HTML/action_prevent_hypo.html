actions to prevent further episodes of hypoglycaemia

Possible cause

Possible action after treating hypoglycaemia
Mismatch between carbohydrate intake and insulin action DO NOT OMIT INSULIN IF DUE but the doses will need review. Review timing of insulin administration, eg premixed insulins need to be administered with the meal.  
Eg abrupt cessation of enteral or parenteral nutrition Review how long the action of already administered insulin will persist, may need iv glucose to cover time of remaining insulin action
Eg NBM but insulin not adjusted Stop bolus insulin with meals while NBM, basal insulin to continue If premixed insulin, switch to basal insulin only
Eg nausea, vomiting Review symptom management, Review nutrition, dietitian review.  Consider reducing insulin doses by 10-30%
